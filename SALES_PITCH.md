## What We Built

**Assignment Pipeline** is an AI-powered web application that transforms how teachers design and validate assignments. It combines:

### Two Integrated Workflows:
1. **Create**: AI-assisted assignment generation with Bloom's taxonomy alignment
2. **Analyze**: Intelligent feedback on existing assignments from 11 distinct perspectives

### The Unique Value: 11-Perspective Feedback System
- **6 Learning Styles**: Visual, Auditory, Reading/Writing, Kinesthetic, Analytical, Social/Emotional
- **5 Accessibility Profiles**: Dyslexia, ADHD, Visual Processing, Auditory Processing, Dyscalculia

Teachers see exactly how different students will experience their assignment **before it hits the classroom**.

### Smart Analysis Engine
- 15+ quality markers detected automatically
- Actionable improvement suggestions with AI rewriting
- Before/after comparison with quality metrics
- Rubric generation and Bloom's alignment tracking
